## KEY RULES FOR SLACKBOT
- **Always use Puzzle Cards** — BeastBot gives way better hints per card
- **500 char limit** per message
- **Be specific** — mention exactly what you found and where
- **If answer isn't a location, you're not done** with that puzzle
- **Create cards even for uncertain finds** — BeastBot will tell you if it's a puzzle
